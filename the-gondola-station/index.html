The Gondola Station

Three design concepts for a new gondola station focus on transit-oriented development and community integration, with variations in how they handle gondola placement and building arrangements.

The planning process for a new gondola station in Telluride’s town center is in its early stages. A central concern is that without approval of a Home Rule Charter amendment, voters will not have direct input on the project’s final approval.

This post examines the three proposed designs and advocates for greater community participation in the gondola station development process—one of the most significant infrastructure decisions Telluride will make in the coming decade.
