Livable Telluride ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website livabletelluride.org (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our Site.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information from visitors. Specifically:

Information You Provide: If you voluntarily submit a correction suggestion, subscribe to our newsletter via Mailchimp, or use the "Suggest a Source" feature, we may collect your name and email address. We do not require account creation to use the Site.

Automatically Collected Information: Like most websites, our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) may automatically collect basic server log information, including your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. We do not use cookies for tracking purposes. We do not use any third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics).

2. How We Use Information

Any information collected is used solely to operate and improve the Site, respond to correction suggestions, deliver newsletter updates you have opted into, and ensure the Site functions properly. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

The Site may integrate with or link to third-party services, including:

GitHub Pages: Our Site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub's privacy practices are governed by GitHub's Privacy Statement.

Mailchimp: If you subscribe to our newsletter, your email address is managed through Mailchimp. Their privacy practices are governed by Intuit's Privacy Statement.

Facebook: The Site embeds a Facebook Page Plugin from our Facebook page ("The Livable Telluride"). When this embed loads, Facebook may collect information according to Meta's Privacy Policy. We use a Meta Developer App (App ID: 27540761328856884) solely to embed and display public content from local community organizations.

External Links: The Site contains links to government websites, news sources, and community organizations. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

4. Community Pulse & Public Content

The Community Pulse section aggregates publicly available posts from local organizations, clubs, nonprofits, and community groups. We only display content that has been made public by those organizations on their own social media accounts or websites. We do not collect private messages, private group content, or non-public information.

5. Data Security

The Site is a static website with no database or user authentication system. We take reasonable measures to protect any information submitted through the Site. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To unsubscribe from our newsletter, use the unsubscribe link in any email we send. For other requests, contact us using the information below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of the Site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
